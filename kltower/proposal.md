**Status:** Proposal — Pilot Project

## Executive Summary

"Gaming in the Sky" is a first-of-its-kind international esports and technology festival proposed for Menara Kuala Lumpur (KL Tower). The event transforms Malaysia's most iconic landmark into a 3-day convergence of competitive gaming, technology innovation, and international tourism — positioning KL Tower as a bold new destination in the global esports calendar.

This pilot project targets 5,000+ attendees across three days, featuring international esports tournaments (MLBB & Honor of Kings), a hackathon, tech expo, LAN party, and broadcast production — all set against the dramatic backdrop of KL Tower.

**Budget range:** RM 500,000 — RM 1,000,000 (scalable packages)

## Event Pillars

### 1. International Invitational Esports
- **Games:** Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) + Honor of Kings (HOK)
- **Format:** Malaysia vs Philippines vs Indonesia — invitational bracket with group stage and grand finals
- **Includes:** Prize pool, professional casters, analysis desk, team logistics
- **Impact:** Headline attraction driving international attention and streaming viewership

### 2. Community Open Tournaments
- **Games:** MLBB, Honor of Kings, PUBG Mobile, EA FC 26, eFootball
- **Format:** Open registration (walk-in + pre-reg) — Swiss/bracket format per title across main and side stages
- **Includes:** Prize pools per title, stage rotation schedule, caster coverage, livestream per match
- **Impact:** Mass public participation; drives footfall, social media buzz, and grassroots community engagement

### 3. Hackathon in the Sky
- **Format:** 48-hour hackathon at KL Tower's observation deck
- **Themes:** Gaming tech, AI applications, tourism-tech prototypes
- **Includes:** 20 teams, industry mentors, demo day, prizes
- **Impact:** Innovation positioning; generates media interest and corporate partner engagement

### 4. Mini Super Tech Expo
- **Format:** Exhibition hall with 30+ booths
- **Features:** VR/AR demos, gaming peripherals, AI showcases, indie game stations, hardware showcases, startup booths, product launches
- **Collaboration:** Access to WirForce ecosystem sponsors (ROG, GIGABYTE, Seasonic) for hardware showcase partnerships
- **Impact:** Revenue-generating through booth rentals; delivers hands-on experiences for attendees

### 5. LAN Party in the Sky
- **Format:** BYOC-inspired LAN gaming zone with 100+ PCs, modelled after WirForce's proven multi-day LAN format
- **Features:** Casual tournaments, BYOC play, PC modding showcase, retro gaming corner
- **Collaboration:** 4Gamers/WirForce consultation on LAN zone layout, BYOC logistics, and multi-day gaming operations
- **Impact:** Community engagement; longest dwell-time activation driving F&B and merchandise revenue

### 6. Tourism & Hospitality Package
- **Includes:** Airport transfers, hotel partnerships, KL city tours, halal food trails, VIP lounge
- **Impact:** Positions event as a tourism driver; strengthens KL Tower's international visitor proposition

### 7. Livestream, Talent & Broadcast Production
- **Includes:** Multi-camera setup, live streaming (YouTube, Facebook Gaming, TikTok), professional casters, hosts, analysts, floor MCs, replay systems, on-screen graphics, post-event highlight reels and content packages
- **Impact:** Extends reach to 50K+ livestream viewers; creates permanent content assets; professional talent elevates broadcast quality

### 8. Opening / Closing Ceremony
- **Includes:** VIP speeches, LED countdown, cultural performances, light shows, awards ceremony, after-party
- **Impact:** Creates premium moments for media coverage and sponsor activation

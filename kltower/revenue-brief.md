# Gaming in the Sky @ KL Tower — Realistic Revenue Brief (v2)

**Format:** 3 days / 2 nights
**Currency:** Ringgit Malaysia (RM)
**Pricing basis:** 2026 KL/SEA market rates, calibrated against MY paid-event attendance benchmarks (MPL Finals, MGS, Comic Fiesta, Level Up KL, WirForce TW).
**Status:** Year 1 pilot — pricing built for franchise establishment, not maximum extraction.

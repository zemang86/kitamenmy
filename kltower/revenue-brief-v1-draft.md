# Gaming in the Sky @ KL Tower — Revenue Brief (v1)

**Format:** 3 days / 2 nights
**Currency:** Ringgit Malaysia (RM)
**Scope of this doc:** All revenue streams **excluding** corporate sponsorship (covered separately — see costing-brief partnership model). Sponsorship is the largest revenue chunk; this doc plans everything else to ensure profitability across **3 volume scenarios: Conservative / Moderate / Optimistic**.

---

## Revenue Strategy Overview

### Core Principles
1. **Bundle KL Tower observation deck access into every public ticket** — adds tourism value, justifies premium pricing, drives venue affinity.
2. **Activation-based entry fees** for tournaments, hackathon, and LAN — each is its own product with its own price ladder.
3. **Forced inclusions where attendee dwell time is high** — Hackathon (48hr) and LAN Party (60hr) MUST include F&B in price; not optional.
4. **Tiered upsells** — every activation has at least 2 price points (basic / premium).
5. **Tourism + hotel bundles** for international visitors leveraging Tourism Malaysia partnership.

### Critical Observations
1. **Sponsorship is the make-or-break category.** Conservative scenario fails because sponsorship is underweight. Securing **at least 1 Title + 3 Gold** (RM 850K minimum) is essential for break-even.
2. **Tickets (§A) drive 15–25% of revenue** — pricing is calibrated for KL premium positioning. Bundling KL Tower observation deck is the differentiator vs. standard convention pricing.
3. **Booth/expo (§F) is the second-largest non-sponsorship stream** — drives RM 176K–412K range. Critical to fill 90%+ of booth inventory.
4. **Merchandise margin is significant** — RM 60K–240K net. A real merch program (not just T-shirts) at scale.
5. **Tourism bundles + hotel commissions (§I)** are the upside path with Tourism Malaysia partnership — could double if AirAsia/MAS comes on as travel partner.

## Risk & Sensitivity Notes

### Pricing Risks
- **Price elasticity for GA tickets:** RM 89 is at the upper end of MY esports event pricing. Test with early-bird at RM 69.
- **VIP saturation risk:** Sky Box / Sky Deck capacity caps VIP scaling. Don't over-promise.
- **LAN BYOC pricing (RM 299) is premium** — must be justified by network quality + F&B inclusion. WirForce TW reference: NTD 1,500-2,500 (~RM 215-360).

### Volume Risks
- **5,000 attendees over 3 days = ~1,700/day average.** Realistic for a pilot but requires strong marketing (§K of costing-brief).
- **Hackathon at 20 teams is aggressive** for a paid hackathon in MY (most are free/sponsor-funded). Consider sponsor-subsidized teams.
- **Community tournament fill rate** — 128 teams per title is optimistic without prize pool of RM 30K+. Conservative 32-team scenarios are safer.

### Operational Risks
- **F&B vendor revenue share** depends on vendor mix and pricing. Lock vendor agreements 2 months pre-event.
- **Merch logistics** — pre-order vs. onsite stock. Onsite stockouts kill margin; over-stock kills cash flow. Test demand via pre-event drops.
- **Tourism bundles** depend entirely on Tourism Malaysia / airline partnership being signed early.

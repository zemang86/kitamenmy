Why It Works

NTU Cost Advantages

Campus partnership fundamentally changes the cost equation for a festival of this scale.

$0 Venue Rental

NTU provides auditoriums, lecture halls, and green spaces — eliminating the largest single cost for any large-scale event.

$0 Hardware (BYOC)

Gamers bring their own rigs — at 10K scale, that's $2M+ worth of equipment the festival doesn't need to provide.

Existing Infrastructure

Campus power grid, Wi-Fi backbone, and security systems provide a foundation that commercial venues can't match.

Student Volunteers

NTU's volunteer programme reduces professional staffing costs by 30–40% while building campus ownership of the event.

Risk Management

Contingency Planning

Every major risk has been anticipated with built-in mitigation strategies.

Weather (Outdoor Zones)

Indoor backup venues available across NTU campus — lecture halls and covered walkways can absorb outdoor programming.

Sponsor Shortfall

Tier 1 (The Launch) designed to be viable with minimal sponsorship — core features run independently of sponsor funding.

Attendance Below Target

Scalable programming — reduce zones without waste. BYOC seating and activations flex down gracefully.

Technical Failure

Redundant power and networking at Full Scale tier. NOC team monitors infrastructure 24/7 during the event.

Budget includes ~5% contingency buffer within Operations & Security.
